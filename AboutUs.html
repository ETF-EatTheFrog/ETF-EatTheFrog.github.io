About Us

Our Mission

Being college students ourselves, we understand that there is a lot to do every day. But this doesn’t mean, we get lost in things. We can reclaim our time by proper time management. Eat the Frog's mission is to allow yourself to take back control of your time by better organizing your work and life.

Our Reviwers

Why User's can trust Us? Hear from our reviewers.

Our team has a combined 10 years working withing the IT industry and regularly consults with other professional to make sure our products provide the most value to our users. As both students and working professions ourselves we understand the needs and problems our users face every day. When our users place their trust in us to help solve their problems, we believe it is our duty to respect and honor that trust by working to ensure our users are provided with the best possible experience we can provide. Our users trust is the most valuable thing they can give us and we strive to do our upmost to return that value to them in the form of our product.

The website seems really easy to use and I was able to learn how to use it within a day. I found the app because I needed to study for semester finals and enjoyed it so much, I started using it for my other tasks as well.

Was presently surprised at how easy the site was to navigate. I tried several other apps and found them hard to use so I was a little scared that this would be another bad apple. Thankfully, I was able to navigate the site far more effectively than I originally though.

Really liked the split between physical and sedentary tasks. It helped me organize my tasks better than usual. I like to play dodgeball on Saturdays but I need to study as well so that division really helped me to get everything together.

Found the hour counts for tasks to be very helpful. I use other digital to-do lists and I keep coming back to this one because of how easy and intuitive it is to use.
